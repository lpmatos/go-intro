# The History and Benefits of Go

Before we get started actually learning how to make tools with Go, i wnat to talk a little bit about the language itself.

## The History

* Development of the language began nt at Google in 2007.
* First public release of Go was in 2009.
* Version 1.0 released in 2012.
* Version 2.0 is currently in design and development. 

## The Benefits of Go

* High performance.
* Static typing with a fast compiler.
* Designed to leverage multi-core systems.
* Highly productive language (developers love Go).
* Rapidly growing in popularity.
* Capable of building self-contained binary packages.
